About Basava Group

Building Businesses. Creating Impact.

Who We Are

Basava Group of Companies is a Lusaka-based, multi-sector organisation operating across construction, financial consulting, and social development. We bring together expertise from diverse industries to deliver high-quality services, drive innovation, and create long-term value for our clients and communities.

Our approach is rooted in professionalism, integrity, and a commitment to excellence. Whether through infrastructure development, financial advisory, or community initiatives, we aim to make a meaningful and lasting impact.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional services across all sectors we operate in while upholding the highest standards of integrity, innovation, and quality. We are committed to empowering businesses and communities through reliable and sustainable solutions.

Our Vision

To build a future where success is shared. We strive to become a trusted and globally recognized group that not only drives business growth but also contributes meaningfully to societal development and collective progress.

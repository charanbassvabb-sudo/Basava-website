Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to register for VAT?

It depends on your annual turnover and the nature of your business activities. We can review your figures against current ZRA thresholds and advise whether VAT registration applies to you.

What's the difference between PAYE, NAPSA, and NHIMA?

PAYE is income tax deducted from employee salaries and remitted to ZRA. NAPSA is the statutory pension contribution, and NHIMA is the national health insurance contribution. All three are typically processed together through payroll.

I run a small business — do I really need an accountant?

Even small and early-stage businesses benefit from proper bookkeeping, since it keeps you compliant, makes tax season painless, and gives you real numbers to make decisions with. We offer packages scaled to business size.

Can you help if my books are already behind or disorganized?

Yes — catching up backlogged records and reconciling past periods is one of our most common engagements. We'll assess where things stand and bring you current.

Do you work with businesses outside Lusaka?

Yes, we support clients across Zambia remotely, with in-person meetings available for clients based in or visiting Lusaka.

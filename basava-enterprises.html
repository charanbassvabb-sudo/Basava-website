About Us

Basava Enterprises Ltd is a construction and engineering company based in Lusaka, Zambia, specializing in steel fabrication and concrete structure development. We deliver reliable and high-quality solutions for industrial, commercial, and infrastructure projects.

Our approach focuses on durability, efficiency, and precision. From planning to execution, we ensure that every project meets the highest standards of safety and quality.

About Basava Group

Basava Group of Companies is a Lusaka-based multi-sector organisation operating across engineering, financial consulting, and social development sectors. We are committed to building sustainable businesses while contributing positively to society through innovation, integrity, and collaboration.

Our Vision

In a world where success is often pursued individually, Basava Group believes in growing together. Our mission is to empower communities, support businesses, and create opportunities that enable collective progress and long-term impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Basava Group offer?

Basava Group operates across three companies: construction and steel/concrete structures through Basava Enterprises, accounting and business advisory through Basava Business Consultants, and community development through the Basava Social Welfare Foundation.

Where is Basava Group based?

We are based at Plot 16797, MassMedia, Lusaka, Zambia, and serve clients across the country.

How do I get a quote for a construction project?

Reach out through our contact page or WhatsApp with a brief description of your project, and our Enterprises team will get back to you to discuss scope and timelines.

Can the different Basava companies work together on one engagement?

Yes. Many clients use more than one of our companies at once, for example construction services alongside financial advisory, and we coordinate across teams to keep things simple for you.
